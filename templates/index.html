Welcome to League Stats

A collection of web applications that allow you to chart data from League of Legends in cool ways!

MasteryAnalysis

A program that analyzes masteries and attempts to find out what the most played champion by your friends is (among other things)

Try it!

ClashScale

A program that creates a scatter plot representing clash games won versus clash games played. Use this to find which one of your friends is the best clash player!

Try it!

ChampionScale

A program that creates a scatter plot representing mastery on a champion versus the level of that summoner. Use this to find who is the most intense insert champion here gamer out of all your friends!

Try it!
